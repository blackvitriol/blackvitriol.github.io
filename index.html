Personal Life

If you have taken the time to read this, I am grateful for your interest. Please, do not mind my humor. There exists something known as "Professional Humor" (or does it?)

My name is Ahmad, and I am a computer scientist and a robotics engineer. The titles that you have read, these are not titles that I have selected myself, rather, they have been inferred upon me by the completion of my formal education and the acquisition of a wide spectrum of skillset that allows me to make this claim. I love my work which is why I take pride in it and I have been working with computers, since the age of 3 ! Growing up in Dubai, I have been tech-savvy, trying to hack and reverse engineer anything I could find, since a very young age. My higher education was based on Information and Communication Technology, and Mathematics. This was followed by a Bachelors degree in Computer Science from Troy State University in Alabama, USA. Fast-Forward. Currently, I am on the verge of completing my thesis, in a Masters programme of 'Robotics and Intelligent Machine Engineering' from National University of Science and Technology in Islamabad, Pakistan.
